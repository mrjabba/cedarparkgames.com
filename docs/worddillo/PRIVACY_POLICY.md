# Privacy Policy for Worddillo

**Last Updated:** April 17, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Worddillo is a word puzzle game developed and operated by the developer. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

## 2. Information We Collect

**Local Storage Only:**
- Worddillo stores your game high scores locally on your device using AsyncStorage
- This data is stored ONLY on your device and is never transmitted to any server
- We do not collect any personal information such as name, email, or location

## 3. How We Use Information

The locally stored high scores are used solely to:
- Display your personal best scores within the game
- Track your game progress and achievements

This data remains on your device and is never shared with third parties.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties because we do not collect any identifiable user data.

## 5. Children's Privacy

Worddillo is appropriate for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the app store listing or your preferred method.

---

*This policy applies to the Worddillo mobile app version 1.0.0*
